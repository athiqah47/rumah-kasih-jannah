About Rumah Kasih Jannah

Learn more about our vision, mission, and the dedicated team behind our efforts.

OUR VISION

To create a nurturing and supportive environment where orphaned children and rescued babies receive the care, love, and opportunities they need to grow, thrive, and lead fulfilling lives.

OUR MISSION

Is to provide essential support, shelter, and education to orphaned children and rescued babies in Malaysia. We are committed to offfering them a safe and loving and loving environment, empowering them with the resources and guidance to build a brighter future. Through community engagement, advocacy, and partnerhips, we aim to improve the well-being of the most vulnerable members of society.
